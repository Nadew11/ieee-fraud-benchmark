Executive Recommendation

We recommend that the XGBoost fraud detection model (v4.2.1) be DEPLOYED with a TUNED classification threshold of 0.644, rather than deployed as-is (with the analyst's default threshold of 0.5) or rejected. Recommended Classification Threshold
------------------------------------
We recommend setting the decision threshold to exactly 0.644. 

Why this threshold?
- Operational Feasibility: Our manual investigation team of 12 analysts has a hard capacity limit of 1,500 alerts/day. At a threshold of 0.644, the model flags approximately 1,480.3 alerts per day. This represents a 98.7% capacity utilization rate, meaning the team can handle the volume without any additional headcount or backlog buildup.
- Cost-Benefit Optimization: Under the constraint that daily alerts must not exceed 1,500, a threshold of 0.644 is the mathematically optimal choice. It minimizes the total quarterly cost to $192.73M. Lower thresholds would generate a volume of alerts that our team cannot review, while higher thresholds would let too many high-loss fraud cases go undetected.
