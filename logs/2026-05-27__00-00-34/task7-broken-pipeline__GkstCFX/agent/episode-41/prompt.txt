1. Data Leakage via StandardScaler (Preprocessing Leakage)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- **Bug**: The pipeline called `scaler.fit_transform(X)` on the entire dataset
  before performing the train-test split via `train_test_split`.
- **Why it is wrong**: This is a classic data leakage bug. Fitting a scaler on the
  entire dataset leaks statistical parameters (mean and standard deviation) from the
  validation set into the training set. This leads to overly optimistic validation
  metrics that will not generalize to unseen test/production data.
